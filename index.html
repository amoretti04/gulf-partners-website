Our Focus

Investment Strategy

01

Growth Capital

We deploy up to USD 200 million in preferred structured capital solutions to mid-market companies across the GCC — and selectively in EMEA — to accelerate expansion, support transformative acquisitions, and facilitate the GCC's economic transformation. Our strategy capitalises on a distinctive opportunity: bridging the gap between available capital and the financing needs of SMEs underserved by regional banking systems, targeting attractive risk-adjusted returns.

02

Selective Co-Investments

We invest as a minority equity co-investor on identical terms — same entry date, same price, same share class — ensuring full alignment with our partners. Every investment must pass a concrete GCC value-creation test.

  • Add-ons We source preferred capital solutions and bolt-on targets from our GCC network for your platforms.
  • Expansion We open KSA, UAE, and wider GCC distribution for portfolio companies.
  • Exit We connect to GCC strategic buyers — family offices, SWFs, and regional corporates.
  • Reciprocal We help our GCC companies expand into Europe on the same terms.

Investment Thesis

Target Sectors & Strategy

The Fund pursues a differentiated cross-border strategy focused on investing in selected niche SMEs across EMEA whose products and services are directly relevant to the long-term transformation of GCC economies.

⚙️

Industrial Tech & Smart Manufacturing

Back specialist providers of automation, industrial software, sensor-based quality control, and maintenance solutions that support productivity and localization in industrial value chains, aligning with GCC diversification ambitions.

🌱

Sustainable Food & Agri Solutions

Back specialist platforms supporting food security through precision irrigation, controlled-environment agriculture, and food supply-chain technology. The strongest opportunities are technology-enabled models with repeat revenues.

🚢

Logistics Tech & Supply Chain

Focus on asset-light supply chain platforms, including warehouse solutions, fleet optimization, last-mile orchestration, and trade-support services. Target companies demonstrate embedded customer relationships and strong scalability.

💧

Water & Resource Efficiency

Target specialist SMEs in water treatment, reuse, monitoring, leakage detection, and industrial water-efficiency technologies. Water security is a strategic priority for GCC economies, creating a compelling opportunity for proven EMEA platforms.

♻️

Circular Economy & Waste Valorization

Focus on businesses active in recycling, packaging circularity, industrial waste recovery, and resource-efficiency services. Attractive companies have proprietary processes, contracted relationships, and the ability to expand through replication.

🏥

Healthcare Services & HealthTech

Invest in focused healthcare platforms that improve access and outcomes, including diagnostics, digital health, and specialty care. Ideal targets are niche leaders with the ability to drive sustainable margin expansion through digital adoption.
