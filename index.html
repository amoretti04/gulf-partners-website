Our Focus

Investment Strategies

01

Growth Capital

Providing strategic capital to established businesses seeking to accelerate expansion, enter new markets, or pursue transformative acquisitions across the GCC and EMEA.

02

Buyout Investments

Preferred structured capital fund that invests in market-leading businesses with strong fundamentals and significant value creation potential, to selectively capture opportunities arising from the GCC economic transformation and sustainability agenda.

03

Venture Capital

Scale-up growth-stage investments in innovative technology companies—particularly leveraging digital tools, data infrastructure, and AI—that are reshaping industries and creating new markets.

04

Real Assets

Strategic investments in infrastructure, real estate, and tangible assets that provide stable returns, portfolio diversification, and directly support national resilience priorities.

Our Approach

Value Creation Through Partnership

Our strategy is centered on scalable, capital-light, and profitable businesses operating in sectors supported by strong structural tailwinds. We believe that sustainable value creation requires deep collaboration, operational enhancement, and technology-led business transformation.

01

Strategic Expansion

We target market leaders with defensible competitive positions, leveraging our deep network to accelerate their growth through strategic expansion into high-demand GCC markets.

02

Operational Excellence

We work alongside portfolio companies to drive operational improvements, implement best practices, and enhance commercial effectiveness to support sustainable margin expansion.

03

Tech & AI Integration

We seek to leverage innovation in digital tools, data infrastructure, automation, and generative AI to improve productivity and strengthen decision-making across the portfolio.

Our People

Experience Meets Innovation

Our team brings together decades of cross-border investment experience with fresh perspectives. United by a commitment to excellence and a passion for building exceptional businesses, we are trusted partners to entrepreneurs, family offices, and institutions worldwide.

Investment Thesis

Target Sectors & Strategy

The Fund pursues a differentiated cross-border strategy focused on investing in selected niche SMEs across EMEA whose products and services are directly relevant to the long-term transformation of GCC economies[cite: 2].

💧

Water & Resource Efficiency

Target specialist SMEs in water treatment, reuse, monitoring, leakage detection, and industrial water-efficiency technologies. Water security is a strategic priority for GCC economies, creating a compelling opportunity for proven EMEA platforms[cite: 1].

♻️

Circular Economy & Waste Valorization

Focus on businesses active in recycling, packaging circularity, industrial waste recovery, and resource-efficiency services. Attractive companies have proprietary processes, contracted relationships, and the ability to expand through replication[cite: 1].

🌱

Sustainable Food & Agri Solutions

Back specialist platforms supporting food security through precision irrigation, controlled-environment agriculture, and food supply-chain technology. The strongest opportunities are technology-enabled models with repeat revenues[cite: 1].

🏥

Healthcare Services & HealthTech

Invest in focused healthcare platforms that improve access and outcomes, including diagnostics, digital health, and specialty care. Ideal targets are niche leaders with the ability to drive sustainable margin expansion through digital adoption[cite: 1].

🚢

Logistics Tech & Supply Chain

Focus on asset-light supply chain platforms, including warehouse solutions, fleet optimization, last-mile orchestration, and trade-support services. Target companies demonstrate embedded customer relationships and strong scalability[cite: 1].

⚙️

Industrial Tech & Smart Manufacturing

Back specialist providers of automation, industrial software, sensor-based quality control, and maintenance solutions that support productivity and localization in industrial value chains, aligning with GCC diversification ambitions[cite: 1].

Investment Impact

Creating Lasting Value

Sustainability acts as both a value-creation lens and a demand driver for our investments[cite: 3]. Through active partnership and operational support, we help these businesses achieve extraordinary growth while aligning with regional transformation mandates.
